Frequently Asked Questions

Grow Your Business Online With The Best Web Development Company!

  • Why do I need web design/develoment services?

    From UI/UX to the codebase, a full stack web development undertaking requires a lot of resources and expertise to build a perfect website. By perfect website, we mean a website that is designed for users, while being optimized for organic search rankings for targeted keywords, while also being efficient in the backend so it loads instantaneously.

  • How much do web design and development service cost?

    Our web design and development services start at 5000Rs/year, but depend entirely on the scale of your project. A single landing page will cost a lot less than a 40-page website, so how much you'll have to pay depends entirely on the scope of your project.

  • How Long Should I expect process to take?

    The short answer is: it depends! Depending on whether you want to create a small website for a store or a large e-commerce website with a lot of pages, the time it takes to execute your project will vary. It can be anywhere from a couple of weeks to a month, but our goal is always to expedite the process as much as possible. Once you give us more details about your requirements, we can provide you with a custom quote regarding your project.

  • What if I am not satisfied with the results?

    Our team works tirelessly to ensure that the website we build for you fulfills all your business goals—we do this on both a technical and copywriting level. We also make as many changes as you require of us, so failure isn't a very high possibility. However, in case you still feel like we fell short, and don't want to use the work we did, you can expect a refund.

  • Can you manage website goind forward?

    Of course! We'd love to take the task of your hands so you can focus on core business processes. As a holistic web design agency, we have the resources to take on the task with ease.

  • Can I get consultation to determine my website scope?

    Of course, if you give us a call/message, our team would be glad to give you a comprehensive consultation that will cover your goals, services/products, and your budget.

  • Will any content go live without my consent?

    Nothing will be posted without your express consent. All changes will be hosted on a mock website so you get a fair idea of what the final design will look like. Once you sign off on it, only then will the new website go live.
